Easy Ice Coffee

Description

Quite simply, an iced coffee is a cold version of your favourite coffee, but it's not simply a coffee that has been left to cool. Like a regular coffee, it's usually a combination of hot espresso and milk. However, ice is added too.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake until foamy.
  3. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.

Then you made it. Nice and easy, huh!
